About the Event

CodeXplosion is a premier 24-hour hackathon hosted by the Artificial Intelligence and Data Science Department of Dhanalakshmi College of Engineering. This event is a vital platform designed to provide students with hands-on experience, fostering collaborative problem-solving and rapid prototyping skills.

Our mission is to broaden technical exposure, facilitate career development through industry engagement, and cultivate leadership skills among participants. We guarantee a responsibly managed event, meticulously coordinated under the supervision of our esteemed faculty to ensure a seamless and enriching experience for all.
